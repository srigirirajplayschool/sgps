Welcome to Sri Giriraj Playschool

At Sri Giriraj Playschool, we are dedicated to creating a fun, nurturing, and enriching environment for your child. We understand the importance of early childhood education and provide a holistic development program that encourages curiosity, creativity, and emotional intelligence.

Our curriculum blends modern educational methods with traditional values to ignite curiosity, encourage creativity, and promote independent thinking.

Director: Priywart Karnani

Our Vision

To be a pioneering institution that nurtures the intellectual, spiritual, emotional, social, and physical growth of every child.

Our Mission

To provide a safe, creative, and stimulating environment where children learn through hands-on experiences.

Our Core Values

  • Innovation: Fostering creativity and curiosity.
  • Safety: Providing a secure environment.
  • Integrity: Teaching honesty and respect.
  • Community: Partnering with parents and teachers.
  • Excellence: Delivering top-quality education and care.
