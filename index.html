Welcome to Sri Giriraj Playschool

At Sri Giriraj Playschool, we are dedicated to creating a fun, nurturing, and enriching environment for your child. We understand the importance of early childhood education and provide a holistic development program that encourages curiosity, creativity, and emotional intelligence.

At Sri Giriraj Playschool, we offer a child-centric learning experience that blends modern educational methods with traditional values. Our curriculum is designed to ignite curiosity, encourage creativity, and promote independent thinking. We believe that the best learning occurs when children are happy and engaged, and we ensure that every day at our school is filled with joy, exploration, and meaningful interactions.

Director: Priywart Karnani

Our Vision

To be a pioneering institution that provides a holistic learning environment, nurturing the intellectual, emotional, social, and physical growth of every child, ensuring they have a bright and successful future.

Our Mission

To provide a safe, creative, and stimulating environment where children are encouraged to explore, learn, and grow through hands-on experiences that promote their natural curiosity and love for learning.

Our Core Values

  • Innovation: Fostering a culture of creativity and curiosity.
  • Safety: Ensuring a safe and secure environment.
  • Integrity: Instilling values of honesty, fairness, and respect.
  • Community: Building strong partnerships between teachers, parents, and the community.
  • Excellence: Striving for excellence in education, care, and emotional support.

Our Programs

  • Playgroup
  • Activity-based learning: Art, Music, Dance, Yoga, Outdoor Play
